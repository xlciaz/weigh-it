Instructions

Follow these steps to weigh the pros and cons of your decision:

  1. Enter a positive aspect of your decision as a pro.
  2. Assign a numerical value (1-10) indicating its importance to you. (1 being it is of little significance, 10 being this factor is greatly important to you)
  3. Repeat the process for each pro, considering them one at a time. Think carefully!
  4. Once you're done, click the "Done" button.
  5. Repeat the same steps for each negative aspect of your decision in the cons section.
  6. Click "Done" and the program will calculate whether your pros outweigh the cons!
